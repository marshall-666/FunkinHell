Funkin Hell is a magazine that features and promotes underground artists and bands, and gives them exposure and audience that we believe they deserve. Any genre of music can be displayed from metal to rap, there will be "no shortage of music or art" that someone when they look at our magazine will appreciate. Funkin Hell is based in Vancouver, but it will feature people from all across Canada, there will be a monthly exhibition that puts what has been featured on the website and magazine on display for all to see. It is an open door exhibition, so anyone will be able to come in and have a good time. Our creative articulation of art forms is a standalone platform where remarkable creativity which is left unheard and unseen is embraced exclusively. It's a place where likeminds come together and create pieces that hold the audience’s attention in awe! Checkout our music, art and explore section or get in touch if you want to see your work featured.
