If you want to exprience Funkin' Hell, we have monthly exhibitions that feature art and musicians from the past month and gives them a platform to grow. The location of the exhibitions changes every month so be on the look out to know where we will be next. It is free for everyone to come and enjoy the music and art, and maybe you may get a new favorite musician or potentally commission an artist for some personal work
Funkin' Hell also has a weekly magazine that offers previews of the exhibitions and shows what may be featured in the future. We also have interviews with those that are featured in the month and why they think Funkin' Hell is a platform that they want to be displayed on.
